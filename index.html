About Me

As a kid, I was always intrigued with creating and building. I had to understand how everything worked. This has become a life-long passion to build what I could buy, despite the added cost. I have worked hard to turn my passion into a craft others can appreciate as much as I do. This site is about my journey as a craftsman, and a chance to showcase the projects I have created.

WoodCraft

From my dad's basement workshop, to more tools than my house can fit

LeatherCraft

Knifemaking

I tried this on a whim, and enjoyed creating something new

Remodeling my house

We bought a fixer-upper, which pushed me to learn all sorts of new skills

This project site is intended to show projects I have built. If you have any questions about any projects seen here, or would like blueprints for them feel free to contact me. Make sure to use proper safety equipment when working.
